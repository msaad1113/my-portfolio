About Engineering Innovations

Founded in 2018, Engineering Innovations specializes in mechanical design, product development, and CAD solutions for industries ranging from aerospace to consumer products.

Our Approach

We combine traditional engineering principles with cutting-edge tools like generative design, topology optimization, and additive manufacturing to create solutions that are both innovative and practical.

Technical Capabilities

  • 3D CAD Modeling (SolidWorks, Fusion 360, Creo)
  • Finite Element Analysis (FEA)
  • Computational Fluid Dynamics (CFD)
  • Generative Design
  • Design for Manufacturing (DFM)
  • Rapid Prototyping
